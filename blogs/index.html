Corporate Housing isn't a term that many are familiar with, nor are they familiar with the benefits. Many real estate owners use Property Managers to manage thier homes to save themselves time and money. But is it really that profitable? Or is their an easier way to generate cashflow and still have time? With this post I had to do some research and analysis. What is the difference between Corporate Housing and Property Management? Which one takes less time and creates more money? When people visit my clients site, these are some of the questions they ask. I found the answers and wrote them in an easy to understand article

I myself was inspired while editing this blog post! As I corrected the grammar and the structure of the article, I began to ponder my own existence. What plan do I have?
